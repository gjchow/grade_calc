Grade Calculator

Input the marks received on any assignment and how much the assigment was worth in the overall scheme.
To add another entry click the "Add" button.
To remove an entry click the "Remove" button.
Note: The remove button will remove the bottom most entry.
Marks can be in percentage form or can be in fraction form.
Percentage of grade can only be in percentage form.
Note: Please do not add the percentage sign.
When all entries are input press "ENTER" or click "Submit" to calculate.
Any entries with no mark or no percentage or any entries with values other than the ones listed above will not be calculated.
